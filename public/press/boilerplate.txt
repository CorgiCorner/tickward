tickward is an open-source countdown timer app for deadlines, launches, trips, and personal milestones. It was built for people who want important dates to stay easy to see without scanning a busy calendar view.

The app turns each date into a clear countdown. Users can group timers into projects and spaces, pin the dates that matter most, and use focus mode when one deadline needs full attention.

Time-zone support is useful for people who travel, live across more than one country, or follow events scheduled somewhere else. tickward runs at tickward.com, is AGPL-3.0 licensed, and can be self-hosted. API, webhooks, and MCP are available for automation.
